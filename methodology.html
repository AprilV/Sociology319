Methodology (Draft)

Research Methods and Sources

Research Approach: Autoethnography

I'm using autoethnography‚Äîstudying my own experience to understand broader social patterns. The idea is that my personal experience of learning to collaborate with AI can reveal insights about the larger phenomenon of the AI literacy divide. It's qualitative research focused on lived experience rather than statistics or surveys.

Why this method works for my question: I have unique access to my own collaboration process. I can document internal thought processes, emotional responses, and subtle shifts in communication that would be hard to observe from outside. The detailed notes I've been keeping make this possible.

Analysis Process

How I'm approaching the data:

  1. Read through all notes systematically - looking for patterns, themes, recurring observations
  2. Identify phases in collaboration - how did trust/communication evolve over time?
  3. Find critical incidents - specific moments that changed my thinking or behavior
  4. Connect to theory - how does my experience relate to Turkle's arguments? Digital divide theory? Other sociological concepts?
  5. Look for broader implications - what does my experience suggest about AI literacy inequality in general?

Week 1 task: Actually do this analysis and write it up for the case study page.

Limitations and Considerations

What this method can and can't do:

  • Can: Provide deep insight into the subjective experience of learning AI collaboration, reveal patterns that might not be visible in survey data
  • Can't: Make broad statistical claims. My experience might not be typical. I already had coding skills, which affects my learning trajectory.
  • Addressing bias: Being reflexive about my own position and advantages. Acknowledging what I bring to the collaboration (tech background, education level, etc.)

The point isn't to say "everyone's experience will be like mine"‚Äîit's to use my experience as a lens to understand the phenomenon of AI literacy inequality. One detailed case can reveal insights that inform broader understanding.
