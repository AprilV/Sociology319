Research Overview

This research examines the digital divide in AI literacythe gap between those who can effectively use artificial intelligence tools and those who cannot or will not adopt them.

Central Question: As AI becomes essential to education, employment, and daily life, how do we ensure equitable access to AI skills across age, income, and education levels?
