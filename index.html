📖 Research Overview

This research examines the digital divide in AI literacy—the growing gap in AI skills, confidence, and access across American society.

⚠️ The Problem

While 88% of organizations now use AI (McKinsey, 2024) and AI skills become essential for employment, 66% of Americans have never tried ChatGPT (Pew, 2025). This isn't just an access gap—it's a skills, confidence, and trust gap.

🎯 Central Question:

As AI becomes essential to education, employment, and daily life, how do we ensure equitable access to AI skills across age, income, and education levels? More critically: What stops 66% from even trying?
