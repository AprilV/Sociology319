Why These Settings Matter

Just as this research explores the AI literacy divide, accessibility settings address the disability divide in technology. According to the CDC, 1 in 4 adults in the U.S. has a disability. Yet most websites aren't designed with their needs in mind.

Offering these controls isn't just about WCAG compliance. It's about human dignity, user autonomy, and acknowledging that technology should adapt to people, not the other way around. This is what bridging divides looks like in practice.
